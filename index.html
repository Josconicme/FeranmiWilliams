About Me

Feranmi Williams is a writer, humanitarian leader, and advocate for ethical innovation whose work bridges faith, recovery, and technology. She is passionate about restoring hope, empowering lives, and advancing humanity through the Gospel and responsible innovation.

As Team Lead of Yourecover, a faith-based evangelical humanitarian team, Feranmi leads initiatives addressing addiction, depression, sexual exploitation, homelessness, and social exclusion. Under her leadership, Yourecover has reached schools, streets, and slum communities carrying the message of Christ alongside practical care and recovery support.

She is also the Founder of Linnexus AI Institute, an organisation focused on Generative AI education and ethics. Through this initiative, she equips students, professionals, and community leaders with the skills and moral grounding needed to apply AI for human progress and positive transformation.
